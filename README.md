# 👥 Customer Behaviour Analysis Dashboard

An interactive Business Intelligence dashboard designed to analyze customer purchasing patterns, subscription metrics, and demographic segment performance. This dashboard helps businesses understand what drives consumer behavior across different age groups, categories, and membership levels.

## 🔑 Key Metrics & KPIs

The dashboard monitors core user engagement and sales metrics at a glance:
*   *Number of Customers:* 3K unique profiles analyzed.
*   *Average Purchase Amount:* $59.54 per transaction.
*   *Average Review Rating:* 3.75 / 5.0 (Customer satisfaction index).

## 📈 Core Analytical Insights & Features

### 1. Subscription & Retention Metrics
*   *Subscription Breakdown:* A donut chart showcases user retention levels. *60.29% (2K)* of the customer base consists of non-subscribed users, while *39.71% (1K)* are active subscribers. 
*   This breakdown allows stakeholders to evaluate the direct impact of subscription perks on average order value.

### 2. Category Performance (Revenue vs. Volume)
The dashboard provides a side-by-side behavioral comparison between total financial footprint and transaction volume across four core product categories:
*   *Clothing:* The clear market leader, driving both the highest total revenue (surpassing $50K) and individual sales count (over 1K).
*   *Accessories:* The second most lucrative segment in terms of revenue and volume.
*   *Footwear & Outerwear:* Represent smaller operational segments, with outerwear holding the lowest share in overall sales count and revenue generation.

### 3. Demographic Segmentation (Age Groups)
Horizontal bar charts evaluate how different generations interact with the platform based on revenue contribution and active customer counts:
*   *Young Adults:* Emerge as the primary demographic driver, leading both the highest revenue generated and the largest unique customer count.
*   *Middle Aged & Seniors:* Show balanced, competitive spending behavior close behind young adults.
*   *Adults:* Form the smallest relative segment in both overall revenue generation and customer count.

### 4. Interactive Data Drilling
A dedicated sidebar panel allows users to segment and filter the entire dashboard dataset seamlessly by:
*   *Subscription Status:* Toggle between Subscriber (Yes/No) datasets.
*   *Gender:* Filter insights across Female and Male consumer groups.
*   *Category:* Isolate performance trends for Accessories, Clothing, Footwear, or Outerwear.
